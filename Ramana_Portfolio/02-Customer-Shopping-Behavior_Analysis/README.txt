# Customer Shopping Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping data to identify sales patterns, customer behavior, and revenue trends.
The analysis was performed using **Excel, SQL (MySQL), and Power BI**, and an interactive dashboard was created to visualize key business insights.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* SQL (MySQL)
* Power BI

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains customer shopping transaction data including:

* Invoice Number
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Product Category
* Quantity Purchased
* Product Price
* Payment Method
* Shopping Mall
* Invoice Date
* Sales Amount

---

## Key KPIs

The dashboard highlights important business metrics:

* Total Sales
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Customers

---

## Analysis Performed

Using SQL and Power BI, the following analyses were performed:

* Sales by Product Category
* Sales by Gender
* Sales by Shopping Mall
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top Customer Contribution

---

## Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes:

* KPI Cards for key metrics
* Category and Mall slicers for filtering
* Category-wise sales distribution
* Gender-based sales comparison
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Mall-wise revenue comparison

## Key Insights

* Clothing and Shoes categories generate the highest sales.
* Female customers contribute slightly more to overall revenue.
* Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon malls generate the highest sales.
* Monthly sales trends show fluctuations throughout the year.
